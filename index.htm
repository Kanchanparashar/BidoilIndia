What's Biodiesel ?

Biodiesel is a renewable, biodegradable fuel that can be used in diesel engines. It is made from vegetable oils, animal fats, or recycled restaurant grease and is non-petroleum based. Biodiesel can be used as a diesel fuel on its own or mixed with regular diesel fuel.
